To bridge from ActiveMQ to another JMS provider use the [JMS bridge](ConnectivityConnectivity/Connectivity/jms-to-jms-bridge.md).

To bridge to another kind of transport completely, use [Camel](http://camel.apache.org) or [ServiceMix](http://servicemix.apache.org)
